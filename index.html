Motto for 2021

IEEE Day is set for its commemoration on 17th of October 2021 in GVPCE under the supervision of IEEE Student Branch GVPCE(A). On this occasion we are set to explore on the theme "Up-Skilling: Going above and beyond". We are ready to spirit everyone up in believing that they can make a difference to their communities by skilling up and investing it for a brighter future. "Up-Skilling: Going above and beyond '' is not just a theme for this year’s IEEE Day. It is a call to action to actively engage and take up initiatives to explore today’s emerging technologies and develop ideas for a better today and tomorrow.Our passion, commitment, desire to envision, innovate, and create are the hallmarks that define our efforts and inspire others to join in . So, keep a note in your calendar and join us to celebrate our biggest joy.
